The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought iron tower that stands 1,063 ft(324m) tall in Paris.

Currently, the Eiffel Tower is the most-visited monument in the world with over 7 million visitors in a year.

Visitors can choose to go up using the Tower lift or the stairs.
There are 1,665 steps to the top.

Transports:

  • Bus: lines 42, 69, 72, 82 and 87.
  • Metro: Trocadero, Ecole militaire.
  • Train: Champ de Mars-Tour Eiffel, line C.
